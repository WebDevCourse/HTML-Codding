Side Note

We're set on making our wines
true to the place they grow.
The winemaking is simple -
good estate grown fruit, organic
& biodynamic, handpicked,
vineyard derived yeasts, no
addiivities or fining, minimal
sulphur at bottling and where
possible, no filtration. Then we
have character.
